### FOUNDER STORY

I've been selling on Amazon for years. Built a seven-figure business. Spent hundreds of thousands on advertising. Managed a catalogue across UAE and Saudi Arabia.

And for most of that time, I was running on instinct — the same way most ecommerce founders do.

Saddl is what happens when you take the analytical rigour of an enterprise data team and apply it to the operational reality of a growing ecommerce brand. Not a SaaS product. Not an agency model. An embedded operating system with people who know how to run it.

### HOW IT WORKS

**Onboarding**
We connect to your accounts, audit the current state across every function, and establish baseline metrics. This typically takes two weeks. By the end, you have a clear picture of where the gaps are and what we're going after first.

**Ongoing Operations**
We run your ecommerce operation on a recurring basis. Ads are managed weekly. Inventory is monitored continuously. Pricing is benchmarked against the market in real time. Returns are diagnosed and acted on. Your financials are reconciled at settlement level every period.

**Reporting**
Every two weeks you receive a unified snapshot of performance across all channels — what happened, why it happened, what we're doing next. No interpretation required. No chasing numbers across platforms.

**Strategic Reviews**
Monthly: we zoom out. What's working at the portfolio level? Where should the next unit of investment go? Which SKUs are margin-positive and underinvested? Which ones are burning budget and need to be paused?

---

### WHAT'S INCLUDED

**Advertising Management**
Full campaign architecture and ongoing optimisation across all channels. Keyword strategy, bid management, negative harvesting, campaign structure, spend efficiency. Inventory-aware and margin-aware at all times.

**Inventory Intelligence**
Real-time stock coverage across every SKU. OOS risk flags, reorder signals, procurement recommendations. We track MOQ, lead times, and velocity so your capital goes into the right products.

**Pricing Intelligence**
Competitor landscape monitoring, market position benchmarking, break-even and margin floor analysis. You always know where your prices stand and what room you have to move.

**Returns Diagnostics**
Root cause analysis by SKU and return reason. Disposition tracking. True cost per return including unrecovered fulfilment fees. We identify whether a returns problem is a listing issue, a product issue, or a fulfilment issue — and we fix the right thing.

**Financial Intelligence**
Settlement-level P&L per SKU. Contribution margin after platform fees, fulfilment costs, allocated storage, promotions, and ad spend. The real number — not the dashboard number.

**Unified Reporting**
Biweekly snapshots. Monthly reviews. All channels, all functions, one view.
